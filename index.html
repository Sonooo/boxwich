About Us

We at Bloxwich Technologies are a team of Engineers / Business minded people having more than 25+ years of experience in Electricals & Electronics, Automotive & Off-Road industry.

  • Y2016: Acquisition of Bloxwich Lighting Business in Malaysia
  • Y2018: Diversified to HVAC industry for retrofits of AHUs in Malaysia
  • Y2020: Exclusive tie-ups with SAIVER for AHU/FCU, EBM Papst for EC Fans & EZ Hot for Heat Pumps
  • Y2022: Formed Bloxwich Technologies Pvt Ltd India with a vision to provide best services in HVAC Industry and Energy Savings Products
  • Expertise in AHUs and Retrofits of AHUs with EC Fans and commercial Heat pumps
  • Support office in Malaysia
  • Our expertise in working with multinationals for Sourcing, Development and Supply Chain Management
